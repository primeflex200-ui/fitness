========================================
  PRIME FLEX - CHANGE APP ICON
  Simple Step-by-Step Guide
========================================

STEP 1: Open Android Asset Studio
----------------------------------
Website: https://romannurik.github.io/AndroidAssetStudio/icons-launcher.html
(Should open automatically in your browser)


STEP 2: Upload Your Logo
----------------------------------
1. Click the "Image" tab
2. Click "Choose File" button
3. Navigate to and select:
   C:\Users\ksair\OneDrive\Pictures\WhatsApp Image 2025-09-10 at 3.30.25 PM (1).jpeg


STEP 3: Adjust Settings
----------------------------------
- Padding: 10-20% (adjust to center your logo nicely)
- Shape: Choose what looks best (None, Circle, or Rounded Square)
- Background: Choose your brand color or transparent


STEP 4: Download Icons
----------------------------------
1. Preview how it looks on different devices
2. Click the "Download" button at the bottom
3. Save the ZIP file (probably goes to Downloads folder)


STEP 5: Extract ZIP File
----------------------------------
1. Find the downloaded ZIP file (check Downloads folder)
2. Right-click > Extract All
3. Choose a location to extract


STEP 6: Copy Icons to Project
----------------------------------
Copy these folders from the extracted ZIP:
- mipmap-mdpi
- mipmap-hdpi
- mipmap-xhdpi
- mipmap-xxhdpi
- mipmap-xxxhdpi
- mipmap-anydpi-v26 (if present)

TO THIS LOCATION:
C:\Users\ksair\Downloads\cursor project prime flex(main prime flex)\project-bolt-github-uarm9gkh\flex-zen-coach\android\app\src\main\res\

Replace existing folders when asked!


STEP 7: Rebuild APK with New Icon
----------------------------------
1. Go back to your project folder
2. Double-click: build-apk-simple.bat
3. Wait 5-10 minutes for build to complete
4. New APK with new icon will be in Downloads folder!


STEP 8: Install on Phone
----------------------------------
1. Send PrimeFlex.apk to your phone (WhatsApp, Drive, etc.)
2. Install it
3. Your new logo will appear on home screen!
